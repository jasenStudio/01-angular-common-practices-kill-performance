Neglecting Ahead-Of-Time (AOT) Compilation

Using Just-In-Time (JIT) compilation in Angular results in slower application performance since it compiles the app in the browser. AOT pre-compiles the app during the build process, reducing the work that needs to be done at runtime.


Problem:


JIT compilation increases bundle sizes and makes the app slower to load and execute.


Solution:
